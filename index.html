Jollof rice recipe

A favourite dish in every west africa home, known for its rich flavour and aroma, Jollof is a delicacy.

Preparation time

  • Takes about 45 minutes in total to prepare
  • A total of 5 minutes for food preparation
  • cooking time is about 40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of long grain parboiled rice
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of ginger, grated
  • 1 can (400g) of diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of curry powder
  • 2 cups of chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and cook until translucent.
  3. Stir in the minced garlic and grated ginger, and cook for another minute.
  4. Add the diced tomatoes and tomato paste, and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Stir in the paprika, thyme, and curry powder.
  6. pour in the chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  7. Reduce the heat to low and add the rice.
  8. Stir once, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.
  9. Fluff the rice with a fork and serve hot.
